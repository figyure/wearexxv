  • THESIS TITLESlide:Safe - A safe day is a great day
  • PROBLEMHow can a mobile app utilize community observations and feedback to increase safe backcountry snow travel practices?
  • DESCRIPTIONSlide:Safe is a mobile experience that will extend avalanche advisories beyond their current and limited structure. This will be achieved through user-generated observations of real-time snow conditions in the backcountry. By pairing existent advisories with these observations Slide:Safe will provide the most comprehensive and timely avalanche reports to date. Snow enthusiasts will be able to more accurately make decisions on A) whether they should venture into the backcountry and, if so, B) where they will have the most successful day. The goal of Slide:Safe is to minimize the amount of avalanche related accidents and deaths by minimizing human-triggered slides.
