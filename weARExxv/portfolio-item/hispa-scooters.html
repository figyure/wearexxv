  • THESIS TITLEFORES
  • PROBLEMHow can golfers benefit physically and mentally utilizing wearable technologies and gamification to keep them engaged?
  • DESCRIPTIONFORES is place for golfers to unite, meet and share experiences. Developed with enough forethought to remain engaging over time, the network aims to capture the interest of both new and regular golfers with stats that are beneficial to both health and their game. Golfers are already very interested in their game stats, adding health stats with incentives will only further their interest in staying engaged in a site created for their passion for golf. Working together between the services of a website, mobile app and wearable technology these separate interactive devices capture everything that is needed for a golfer to stay interested and striving for more in their game and with their health. The five sections of the concept that will provide golfers an engaging experience are Community, Health, Stats, Gamification and Application-Based Round Sharing.
