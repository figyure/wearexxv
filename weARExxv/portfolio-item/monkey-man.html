Dan Wilder is a graphic designer who loves communication through design. He focuses on motion graphics and user experience. Dan is highly motivated and somewhat of a nerd! Personal passions include art, computers, and science.

  • CATAGORYInteractive / App
  • THESIS TITLESo Over It
  • PROBLEMHow can design help to facilitate the process of “getting over” someone in our current age of perpetual social media access?
  • DESCRIPTIONTrying to get over someone is so much more difficult today than in previous generations. In the past, when a relationship ended, you could pretty effectively avoid that person over which you were heartbroken. Not so much these days. Now you can check in on your heartbreaker through a myriad of different apps – and they can contact you through similar channels. The design solution will be a smartphone app and it will attempt to encourage the user to avoid contact with their heartbreaker and get over him or her by using incentives and gamification.
